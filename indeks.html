FAQ (Frequently Asked Question)

What is the shelf life of your spice products?

The shelf life of our spice products typically ranges from 1 to 3 years, depending on the type of spice. To ensure freshness, store them in a cool, dry place away from direct sunlight.

How do I store spices?

To maintain their freshness and flavor, store spices in airtight containers away from heat, moisture, and direct sunlight. Cool, dark cabinets or spice racks are ideal storage locations.

How can I determine the quality of your spices?

We prioritize quality by sourcing from trusted growers. Look for vibrant colors, strong aromas, and the absence of moisture or clumping in our spice products.

What's the difference between whole spices and ground spices?

Whole spices are the whole dried spice, such as cloves or cinnamon sticks, while ground spices are the powdered form, like ground cinnamon or ground cloves. Ground spices are more convenient for quick use, while whole spices often have a longer shelf life and can be freshly ground as needed.

Do you provide guides for using your spices?

Yes, we offer guides on our website to help you make the most of our spice products.

Do your spices contain any allergens or additives?

Our spice products are free from common allergens, such as peanuts, tree nuts, and gluten. We do not add artificial preservatives or additives to our spices.

Are your spices sustainably sourced?

We are committed to responsible sourcing practices and work closely with growers and communities who share our commitment to sustainability. We support fair trade initiatives and eco-friendly practices within the spice industry.

Yes, we offer bulk purchasing options for both individual customers and businesses. Contact our customer service for more information.

Can I purchase your spices online?

Yes, our spices are available for online purchase, and we provide international shipping to bring our products to customers worldwide.
